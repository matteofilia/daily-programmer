Hitbox Program

Description:
Determine whether a line passes through a box.

Input:
x1 y1 x2 y2 - The coordinates of a line.
x1 y1 width height - The coordinates of the bottom left corner of the 
box, and its width and height.

Output:
0 - If the line does not pass through the box.
1 - If the line does pass through the box
